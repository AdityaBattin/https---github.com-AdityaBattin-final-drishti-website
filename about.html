Our Story

The team at Drishti has completed a successful 40-year tenure at SGGSIE&T, with a focus on the arts, science, literature, and photography. As the official magazine club of SGGS, they have successfully published five magazines. The club was established to provide opportunities for students to participate in events and activities that promote their overall development.

  • 4+ Events throught year
  • Mega Exibition of All Commitees
  • 1 Magazine and Many Newsletters

Experience the creative world with Drishti -- a festival featuring events that showcase student talent, including Face Painting, Tare-Jammenpar, Wall Magazine, Moonlit Mic Sessions, and Mega Exhibitions. Drishti connects, inspires and brings together students through art.
Drishti not only serves as a magazine, but also as a source of inspiration for those who love art and want to be part of this vibrant community.
