Drishti Events

"Drishti events are characterized by their joyful, exciting, and engaging nature, leaving a lasting impression on attendees."

FACE PAINTING

Experience the thrill of creativity and self-expression with a 4-hour Face Painting event, where teams of 2 or 3 members can showcase their artistic skills by painting their teammates' faces on a chosen theme, making it an exciting and enjoyable activity for all!

MOONLIT MIC

Drishti invites students to showcase their talents under the stars in the event of "Moon Lit Mic". The stage is yours to sing, act, perform stand-up comedy, storytelling and more. This event is an opportunity for students to make the most of their abilities and memorable night.

TAARE ZAMEEN PAR

Drishti invites schools from across the city of Nanded to participate in an artistic extravaganza, a drawing competition. This event is not just about showcasing artistic talents, but also an opportunity to immerse in the beauty of SGGS picturesque campus.

EXHIBITION

The Exhibition, the flagship event of Drishti, provides an opportunity to committees to showcase their talents and speciality to SGGS. It's a visual feast of creativity and innovation, and an amazing chance to see the diverse range of skills and passions of the Drishti community

Drishti Commitees

Drishti has a variety of committees for members to participate in, each with its own specific focus and purpose. These committees offer opportunities for members to explore and develop their interests and skills, and to contribute to the community through their unique specialties and use cases.

Purvarang - Offical Newsletter

Purvarang is a student-run newsletter that aims to provide comprehensive coverage of the happenings at the Shri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology (SGGSIE&T) in Nanded, India. The publication covers a wide range of topics, including academic updates, extracurricular activities, events, and achievements of the students and faculty. The newsletter is written and produced by a group of students who work together to gather information, conduct interviews, and compile the content for each issue.

The goal of Purvarang is to serve as an informative and engaging resource for the SGGSIE&T community, keeping students, faculty, and staff informed about the latest developments on campus.

Editions

Purvarang has successfully issued 7 newsletter editions to date and will continue to highlight our institute's accomplishments.
